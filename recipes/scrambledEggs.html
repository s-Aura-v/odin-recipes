Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled Eggs is one of the most common type of breakfast meal. It is easy to make, rich in protein and can be customized to fit the taste preference of many.

My personal opinion is that they aren't very tasty, but I still eat them. I should learn to properly cook them.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk, plant milk, or water
  • Extra-virgin olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs: Place them in a medium bowl, and whisk until the yolk and whites are thoroughly combined.
  2. Gently preheat the pan
  3. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes
  4. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
