Lemonade

Lemonade is a sour drink that many people enjoy. It is most beloved during the summer as it serves to beat the summer heat with its cool refreshment.

Lemonade are usually made with two essential ingredients: lemon and water.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Pour a glass of water
  2. Squeeze one or two lemon on top of the water
  3. Add sugar to accomodate the sour taste to your liking
